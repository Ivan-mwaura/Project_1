ABOUT US

Agribiz limited is a digital trading platform that mainly focuses on the agricultural sector in its trading means The trading platform was founded by Mr Evan Gachanja with its main mission as to promote the agricultural sector in kenya. Agribiz as a trading platform enables producer consumer cycles in the most efficient manner by connecting farmers to manufactures and also to direct consumers of agricultural products and also creates an intermediary between farmers and buyers with the help of brokers who simplifies the task by acting as link between them.They also deliver the good to buyers at a fixed fee. The mission statement of this website was to improve the economy of kenya by gearing the agricultural sector which is a backbone to kenya`s economy.
To become a partner of this platform the website provides a registration tab where one can sign in as a farmer, a buyer,a broker or as a direct consumer of the products.This has all been simplified in order to get the taste and preference of everybody registered on the website and deliver his trading satisfaction. To sign in one has to fill in his personal details which includes location details,contact information among others.
